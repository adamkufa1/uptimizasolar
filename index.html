The Process

How It Works

Residential solar is simpler than most people think. Here's how a home solar system actually works.

1

Sunlight Hits the Panels

Photovoltaic (PV) solar panels on your roof absorb sunlight and convert it into direct current (DC) electricity throughout the day.

2

Inverter Converts the Power

An inverter transforms the DC electricity into alternating current (AC) power — the same type of electricity your home already uses.

3

Your Home Uses Clean Energy

The converted energy flows directly into your home's electrical panel, powering your appliances, lighting, and devices with clean solar energy.

4

Excess Goes Back to the Grid

Any energy your panels produce beyond what your home needs is sent back to the utility grid through net metering, further reducing your monthly bill.
